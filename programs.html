Our Programs

UNICEF focuses on a variety of programs aimed at improving the lives of children around the world.

Emergency Response

Providing immediate aid such as food, clean water, shelter, and medical care to children affected by emergencies.

Education

Ensuring that every child has access to quality education, even in the most challenging situations.

Child Protection

Working to protect children from violence, exploitation, and abuse.
